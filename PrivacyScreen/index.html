PrivacyScreen Demo

Both iOS and Android have app switchers which show a screenshot of your app. For most apps this is cool, but if your app displays sensitive information this is a potential privacy risk.

This plugin blacks out your app (Android) or shows its splashscreen (iOS) when switching between apps.

Custom plugins don't work in the simulator and the AppBuilder companion app, so inside Telerik AppBuilder choose Run - Build - iOS/Android - App package.

On a real device, push your app to the background and check the running apps.


iOS

Tap the Home button twice, you should now see the splashscreen of your app.


Android

Hold the Home button, you should now see a black screen instead of a screenshot.

Also note that creating a screenshot is no longer possible on Android. Verify this by pressing the Home and Power buttons at the same time.
